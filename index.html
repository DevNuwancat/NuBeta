FAQ

    What makes Sri Lankan gems unique?

    Sri Lanka is renowned for producing some of the world's most exquisite gems, including sapphires, rubies, and various colored varieties. The island's geological conditions contribute to the formation of gems with exceptional clarity, color, and brilliance.

    What is the difference between natural, treated, and synthetic gemstones?

    Natural gemstones are formed through natural processes without human intervention. Treated gemstones undergo enhancement processes to improve their appearance or durability. Synthetic gemstones are created in a laboratory but possess the same chemical composition and physical properties as natural gemstones.
